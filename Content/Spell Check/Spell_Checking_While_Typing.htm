Spell Checking While Typing

This is a feature that lets you keep tabs on your spelling as you type in your content files (e.g., topics, snippets).

how to spell check while typing
  1. Open a content file, such as a topic or snippet.
  2. Select a segment.
  3. Place your cursor in the text edit area at the bottom of the editor.

    • Ribbon In the Spell Check section select Spell Check While Typing.

    • Menu Select Tools>Spell Check While Typing.
  5. Type text in your file. Questionable words are underlined with a jagged red line.
  6. To take action on a questionable word, right-click on it. Then select one of the following from the menu.
    • Alternate spelling suggestions are listed in bold at the top of the menu. If you want to use one of those spellings instead of the current spelling, select the new word.
    • If you want to keep the spelling of the current word and ignore all additional instances of the word in the file (i.e., the jagged red line will be removed from the words, but the words will remain in the file), select Ignore All. Additional occurrences of the word in other topics will still be flagged as a questionable spelling if you use spell check in those files.
    • If you want to keep the spelling of the current word and you do not want to flag it as a questionable spelling again in any of your content files, select Add To Dictionary.
warning

Although this feature can be useful, it may also be a drain on your memory resources. Therefore, you may want to try disabling this option if you experience performance issues with .
