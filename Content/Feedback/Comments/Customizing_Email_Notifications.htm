Customizing Email Notifications

Using the Email tab in the Feedback Admin you can customize the email notifications that users receive related to comments.

how to customize email notifications
  2. In the Feedback Admin, select the Email tab.

  3. In the top area of the tab, double-click on the email you want to customize.

    The Customize Email Message dialog opens.

  4. In the dialog you can edit the subject and/or text body of the email. You can type text, edit HTML tags, and insert special commands (subject and body options).

    • Subject Options These are commands that serve as placeholders for generated content in the subject line. To insert a subject option, place your cursor in the Subject field, click Subject Options at the top of the dialog, and choose an option from the menu. When a subject option is missing from the subject line, it will be active under the Subject Options menu. If a subject option has already been added, it is disabled in the menu. These options are not required.

    • Body Options These are commands that serve as placeholders for generated content in the body of the email. To insert a subject option, place your cursor in the Body field, click Body Options at the top of the dialog, and choose an option from the menu. When a body option is missing from the body text area, it will be active under the Body Options menu. If a body option has already been added, it is disabled from the menu. Some body options are required and will generate errors if they are missing. These errors are displayed in the Issues area at the bottom of the dialog.

  5. (Optional) If necessary, you can restore the email to the default subject and body. To do this, click Options at the top of the dialog and select Restore default.

    Also, if any problems arise (e.g., you have removed a body option that is required), they will be displayed in the Issues area at the bottom of the dialog. You can fix issues by clicking Options and selecting Resolve issues.
