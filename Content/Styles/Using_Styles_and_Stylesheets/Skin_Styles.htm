Skin Styles

In addition to regular and table stylesheets, you can use skin styles. These let you control the look of the online output window (e.g., navigation pane, TOC or browse sequence entries, index keywords). There are three kinds of skins:

  • Standard Skins for , , , , and outputs.
  • HTML5 Skins for output.
  • WebHelp Mobile Skins for output.

See Standard Skin Styles, HTML5 Skin Styles, and WebHelp Mobile Skins.

When it comes to the Standard skin, please note that the vast majority of these skin styles pertain to , , or output. But some of the settings (those that have to do with MadCap Feedback and toolbar items) also apply to . output only supports skin style changes for toolbar items.
