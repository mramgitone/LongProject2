Viewing Frame Links

  • Broken links are drawn with a red color.
  • Links of the selected frame are drawn wider than the links for other frames.
  • Links to sections (i.e., other movies in a collection) are drawn to a small box with the section name.
  • You can double-click a frame to open it in the Frame Editor.
