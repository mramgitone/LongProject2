Moving Sections of an Audio File

After you record an audio file, you can edit the file. One way to do this is to move a section of the audio file from one place to another in the file.

how to move a section of an audio file
  1. Open the movie.
  2. Open one of the following, depending on the element for which you have recorded audio.
    • The properties dialog for an object (double-click the object)
    • The Frame Properties dialog (double-click the frame)
    • The Movie Properties dialog (select Movie>Properties in either the ribbon or menu interface)

  3. Select the Audio tab. The recorded audio file should be displayed in the Audio field.

  4. Click Edit. The Sound Editor opens.

  5. Click in the audio file display and drag the mouse to select an area.
  6. Click .
  7. Specify where you want to move the selected area. You can do this in one of the following ways.

    • You can click once in the display to show the playhead (the red vertical line that displays when you click in the Sound Editor). When you paste the section, it will be inserted immediately after the playhead.

      OR

    • You can click and drag to select an area. When you paste the section, it will replace the specified area.
  8. Click .
  9. In the Sound Editor, click OK.
  10. In the properties dialog, click OK.
