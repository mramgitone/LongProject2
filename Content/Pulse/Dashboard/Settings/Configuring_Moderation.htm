Configuring Moderation—Rejection Reasons

Configuring Moderation

As an Administrator, you can configure your moderation settings by adding reasons for rejecting posts. Then when you reject a post, you can select any of the reasons you've added.

How to Add a Rejection Reason
  1. In the Settings page, click Moderation. The Moderation page opens.
  2. At the bottom of the page, click Add Rejection Reason.
  3. In the Reason field, enter the text that you want.
  4. Click Save. The text is added to the list of reasons.
 note

You can edit a reject reason by selecting it from the list and clicking Configure. You can delete a rejection reason by clicking Remove.
