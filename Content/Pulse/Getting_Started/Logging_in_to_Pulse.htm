Logging In to Pulse

Once you have registered for a account, you can log in to the dashboard or you can log in to the published output to use the community features.

how to log in/out from a topic feed

Logging in to the Dashboard

You can also log in to the Pulse dashboard from a web browser.

how to log in to the pulse dashboard

  1. In a web browser, go to the Site URL for your server.

    If you do not know the Site URL, see the Administrator who installed your Pulse server. Alternatively, if you have access to a Flare target that has already been integrated with the Pulse server, you can open the Community tab and find the URL listed there.

    This opens the Pulse Account Login dialog.

  3. (Optional) Place a check mark in the Keep me signed in box. When selected, will remember the account information that you used to log in and won't ask you to enter your credentials again as long as you are using the same browser on the same computer or device.

  4. Click Login.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

troubleshooting login issues

Below are resolutions to issues that users might encounter when attempting to log in to .

if you have not been given access to pulse

If you attempt to log in to and see the "You have not been given access to Pulse" message, your account has likely been disabled by a administrator. You should contact the administrator for assistance.

if you have an invalid user name or password

If you attempt to log in to and see the "Invalid User Name or Password" message, you may have entered your email address or password incorrectly. Enter the correct email address and password and try again.
