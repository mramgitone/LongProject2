Messaging

Users can take advantage of messaging in a variety of ways, such as general messaging tasks, checking notifications, and sending chat messages.

  • Messaging Tasks The Messages app gives you the ability to read, send, and hide conversations with other users. You must access the Messages app from the dashboard. Messages are not accessible from the output. See Messaging Tasks.
  • Check Notifications Do you want to receive notifications when a users posts an answer to one of your questions? Or be notified when someone asks a question about a particular topic? provides users with a convenient way to sign up to receive email notifications about different system events and activities. See Checking Your Notifications.
  • Send Chat Messages Chat features are available to all registered users. You can initiate a chat if you are a member of the Customers group. Members of the Administrators and Employees group have additional chat options. See Sending Chat Messages.
