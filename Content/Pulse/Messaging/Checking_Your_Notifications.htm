Checking Your Notifications

Do you want to receive notifications when a users posts an answer to one of your questions? Or be notified when someone asks a question about a particular topic? provides users with a convenient way to sign up to receive email notifications about different system events and activities.

Notification Types

notification types

provides users with these notification types:

  • Comments See Responding to Posts.
  • Answers See Posting Questions and Responding to Posts.
  • Mentions See Using Mentions.
  • Invites To learn how to send invites to users, see Inviting People.
  • Tasks Notifies you when you are assigned a task to complete. See Assigning Tasks.

Checking Notifications

allows you to check your notifications.

how to check your notifications

  1. Open the Notifications app using one of these options:

    • Flare Output In a web browser, navigate to the output. Then log in to the system (see Logging In to Pulse). Click the Community tab in the output. Under the Your Apps list, click Notifications.

      notes

      The Your Apps list in the only available to members of the Administrators and Employees groups. It is not available to members of the Customers group.

    • Pulse Dashboard Log in to the dashboard (see Logging In to Pulse). Then click in the upper-right corner of the dashboard. In the drop-down message, click View All Notifications.

  2. In the Notifications page, all of your notifications are listed. You have these options:

    • Click one of the hyperlinks in the notifications page to open the corresponding feed.

    • Click one of the buttons in the upper-right corner of the page to filter your notifications by type.

Signing Up for Email Notifications

signing up for email notifications

When issues a notification, it appears in the Notifications app. If you would like to send you an email message as well, enable the appropriate setting in the Notifications tab on the Settings page of your profile. See Creating and Editing Your Profile.
