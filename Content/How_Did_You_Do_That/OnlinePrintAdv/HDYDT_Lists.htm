How Did You Do That? Lists

This template contains a topic named "Lists" that contains many different kinds of lists that you can use or re-create in your own topics.

Creating these lists involved the following:

  • CSS Styles We created and modified styles to give each list a particular look. For bulleted lists, this included associating the style with a custom bullet image.
  • Creating Lists and Applying Styles Once the styles were created, inserted the lists and applied styles to them where necessary.
types of lists

Here are the kinds of lists found in our template:

  • Simple Numbered List

  • Simple Bulleted List

  • Lists With Paragraphs Between Items

  • Multi-level Numbered List

  • Multi-level Bulleted List

  • Multi-level List (Mixed)

create lists and apply styles

  1. Place your cursor in the document where you want to insert a list. Then do the following, depending on the type of list:

    simple numbered list
    1. In the Paragraph section click the down arrow next to the list button and select Numbered List.
    2. After providing the content for a list item, press Enter. Then enter the next list item text, and so on. To exist the list format, press Enter again on a blank list item.
         simple bulleted list
    1. In the Paragraph section click the face of the list button . Alternatively, you can select the down arrow next to it and select Bullet List.
    2. After providing the content for a list item, press Enter. Then enter the next list item text, and so on. To exist the list format, press Enter again on a blank list item.
         list with paragraphs between items
    1. Follow any of the steps in this topic for creating a particular kind of list.
    2. Click in the list where you want to add a paragraph item. For example, if you want to add a comment between steps 2 and 3, place your cursor at the very end of step 2.
    3. In the Paragraph section click the down arrow next to the List Actions button .
    4. Select Make Paragraph Item(s). A <p> tag is added after the <li> tag.
    5. To add lines without a number or bullet, simply press Enter on your keyboard and type your content.
    6. To continue the numbering or the bullets, click the paragraph item icon . Doing this simply starts another <li> tag.
