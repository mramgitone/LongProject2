Editing Administrator Settings

You can add administrators to the Feedback Admin in order that they may receive email notifications when certain user activity takes place in your output (see Adding Administrators). If necessary, you can edit the property settings for a particular administrator to change the email address and/or email notifications.

how to edit administrator settings
