steps
  1. Start Projects Create a project from scratch, or start a project by importing existing content from a variety of sources. See Starting a Project.
  2. Add "Stuff" Add content and elements to your project, such as topics, text, a table of contents, cross-references, navigation, page layouts, and all of the other "stuff" necessary to help your end users. See Adding Stuff to the Project.
  3. Make Your Project Look Good Through the use of elements such as stylesheets and skins, you can develop a look and feel for your output. See Making It Look Good.
  4. Develop Outputs Decide the type(s) of output formats that you want to generate and design targets accordingly to meet your needs. See Developing Outputs.
  5. Build Output Select an output format and generate the file(s) that you will deliver to users. See Building Output.
  6. Distribute Output Make the output accessible to your end users. See Distributing Output.
