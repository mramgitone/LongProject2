Enabling the Magnifier

You can enable or disable the magnifier that appears when you capture new images (using the "Region" method). The magnifier shows a close-up view of the area drawn by your cursor. This allows you to more accurately capture a specific area. However, if you are using a computer that is relatively slow, you may wish to disable the magnifier.

how to enable the magnifier

    • Ribbon Select File>Options.

    • Menu Select Tools>Options.

    The Options dialog opens.

  2. Select the Capture tab.
  3. Click Enable Magnifier so that it has a check mark.
