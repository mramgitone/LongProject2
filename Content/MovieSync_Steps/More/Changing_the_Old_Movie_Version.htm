Changing the Old Movie Version

After opening movie files, you can select a different "old" movie to replace the one currently loaded.

how to change the old movie version
  1. Select File>Change Old Version.
  2. In the Open dialog, click the Browse button, then find and double-click another movie (MIMOV file).
  3. The movie on the left side of the Frames window pane is replaced with the movie you selected.

Now that the movies are loaded, you can align the frames. See Aligning Frames.
