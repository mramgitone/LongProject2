Transferring Properties

how to transfer properties
  1. In the Frames window pane, do one of the following.

    • If you want to transfer the properties for all frames, click in the local toolbar.

      OR

    • If you want to transfer the properties for only the frame that is currently selected, click in the local toolbar.

  2. In the confirmation dialog that opens, click OK.

    The properties (at least those selected in the Properties window pane) are transferred from the old movie to the new movie.
