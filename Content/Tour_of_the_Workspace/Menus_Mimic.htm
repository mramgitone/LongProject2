Draw Trajectories

Displays or hides the trajectory path of the cursor in the frame. When you record a movie, the trajectory can be added automatically to each frame. Click this button if you want to show or hide it.

Master Frame

Opens a submenu with the following options.

  • Master Frames
  • Show All Master Frames Shows all master frame items in the active frame.
  • Show Top Master Frames Shows all top-level master frame items in the active frame.
  • Show Bottom Master Frames Shows all bottom-level master frame items in the active frame.
  • Hide Master Frames Hides all master frame items in the active frame.
