Snippets in Review Packages

Snippets can now be included in review packages. This means that you can open snippets from the Review Packages window pane, as well as from within a topic.

Snippets in the Review Packages Window Pane

You can double-click a snippet file in the grid and it opens in the XML Editor, just as a topic does. Once it is open, you can make changes review it just like you would a topic.

Opening Snippets Within a Topic

Whereas the previous information pertained to the Review workflows only, the following is supported in all workflows in .

To open a snippet from within a topic, right-click on the snippet and select Open Link.
