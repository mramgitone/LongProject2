Inserting Snippets

After you create or add snippets, you can insert them into a . You can do this by using user interface options, which let you search for a snippet, or you can simply drag an existing snippet from the Content Explorer.

Snippets are pre-set chunks of formatted content. You can insert them into a new document. They cannot be inserted into review topics or contribution documents.
