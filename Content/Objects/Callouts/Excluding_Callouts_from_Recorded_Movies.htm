Excluding Callouts from Recorded Movies

When you record a movie, bubble callouts are automatically added to frames when you perform actions. You can use these callouts to provide information or instructions for users as each action is performed in the movie. However, you also have the option to exclude these callouts from automatically being added when you record a movie. If you decide later that you want some callouts in the movie, you can add them manually.
