Excluding Cursors from Recorded Movies

When you record a movie, automatically captures your mouse movements. In your movie frames, this is reflected by the addition of a cursor with a trajectory (path). When the movie is generated and played, the cursor moves just as you moved it when creating the movie. However, if necessary, you can exclude the automatic addition of these cursor movements from movie recordings. If you decide later that you want some cursors and trajectories in the movie, you can add them manually. See Adding Cursors and Setting Trajectories for Objects.
