Removing Targets

Use the following steps to remove a target.

how to remove a target from a collection
  1. Open the Mimic collection.
  2. In the Collection Editor, click the Targets tab.
  3. Select the target you want to remove.
  4. At the bottom of the tab, click Remove.
 how to remove a target from a standalone movie
  1. Open the standalone movie.
  3. Click the Targets tab.
  4. Select the target you want to remove.
  5. At the bottom of the tab, click Remove.
