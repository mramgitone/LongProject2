Positioning Objects

After you insert an object such as an image, text box, or QR code, you can adjust its positioning on the page. This includes the ability to wrap text around an image or float an image outside the frame holding the regular flow of text. See About Object Positioning, Positioning Images, Positioning Text Boxes, and Positioning QR Codes.
