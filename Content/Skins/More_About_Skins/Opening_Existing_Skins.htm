Opening Skins

After you add a skin to your project, it is stored in the Project Organizer under the Skins folder. At any time, you can open a skin and edit its settings in the Skin Editor.

how to open a skin
  2. Double-click the Skins folder. The skin(s) in your project are displayed.
  3. Double-click the skin that you want to open. The Skin Editor opens to the right.
