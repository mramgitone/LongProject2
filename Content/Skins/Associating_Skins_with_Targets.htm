Associating Skins with Targets

After you add a skin to your project and edit the settings, you need to associate it with a target. After you build the target, the output will be displayed in the skin.

how to associate a skin with a target
  1. Open the target from the Project Organizer.
  2. On the General tab of the Target Editor, click the drop-down arrow in the Skin field and select the skin that you want to associate with the target.
