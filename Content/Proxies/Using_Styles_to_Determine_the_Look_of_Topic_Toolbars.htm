Using Styles to Determine the Look of Topic Toolbars

Topic Toolbars

You can use the <Madcap|topicToolbarProxy> style to change the look of the container holding generated topic toolbars.

how to use styles to determine the look of topic toolbars

  2. note

    Some of the necessary style properties can also be changed in the Simplified view in the Stylesheet Editor.
  4. In the Styles section of the editor, select MadCap|topicToolbarProxy.
  6. In the Properties section to the right, you can change a variety of style settings for topic toolbars. You may need to expand a property group (e.g., Block, Box, Border) to see the actual properties within it.
  7. Here are some of the more common properties that you might change.
    • Border below Read more… To change the border line that appears at the bottom of the topic toolbar, expand the Borders property group. Then click in the right column next to border-bottom, set the border width, unit of measurement, color, and/or style in the small drop-down window. You can also use the other cells in this property group to add borders above, to the left, or to the right of the topic toolbar.
    • Padding above Read more… To change the amount of empty space (padding) above the text within the topic toolbar container, expand the Box property group. Then click in the right column next to padding-top, set the padding value in the small drop-down window, and click OK.
    • Padding below Read more… To change the amount of empty space (padding) below the text within the topic toolbar container, expand the Box property group. Then click in the right column next to padding-bottom, set the padding value in the small drop-down window, and click OK.
    • Space above Read more… To change the amount of space above the topic toolbar container, expand the Box property group. Then click in the right column next to margin-top, set the margin value in the small drop-down window, and click OK.
    • Space below Read more… To change the amount of space below the topic toolbar container, expand the Box property group. Then click in the right column next to margin-bottom, set the margin value in the small drop-down window, and click OK.

    8. notes

    You can also affect the look of topic toolbars by modifying the styles in your skin. See Standard Skin Styles and WebHelp Mobile Skins.
