About Topic Ratings

  • Viewing topic ratings After users enter ratings for topics, you can view them in Feedback Explorer. See Viewing Topic Ratings.
  • Resetting topic ratings When you view topics visited by users, some of the columns in the window pane display ratings information for each topic (i.e., average rating, number of ratings). You can reset the ratings information so that the counter starts over at 0. See Resetting Topic Ratings.
