Positioning QR Codes

After you insert a QR code, you can adjust its positioning on the page. This includes the ability to wrap text around a QR code or float a QR code outside the frame holding the regular flow of text.

how to position a qr code
  1. Select the Position tab.
  2. In the Position section, you can select a Float and a Clear setting. You can also set the Vertical Alignment of the image.
