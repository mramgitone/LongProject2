Contributions Window Pane

This window pane lets you work with contribution documents and templates. See About Reviews and Contributions.

There are two different views in the window pane. The information in the window pane changes, depending on the view you select.

  • Contributions Read more… This view shows contribution topics that you have received from others.
  • Templates Read more… This view shows contribution templates that you have created.

To switch from one view to another, click the drop-down field at the top of the window pane and make your selection.

Following are the primary tasks that you can perform in this window pane.

  • Contribution Topics (View and Accept) Read more… After an individual emails you an MCCO contribution topic, you can bring it into your project, where you can view and accept it. After accepting the topic, it is converted to an HTM file (just like your regular topics) and added to your project. See Viewing Contribution Files and Accepting Contribution Files.
  • Contribution Templates (Send) Read more… You can quickly send one of your contribution templates to someone. That person can then use your template to create a new document and send it to you. See Sending Contribution Templates.
